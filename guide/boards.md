# Using Boards in Zepel
{: .no_toc }

Boards in Zepel give every team in your squad a workflow tailor-made just for them.
{: .fs-6 .fw-300 }

Depending on your squad and the workflows it requires, you can choose to create multiple Boards within your squad to move work items across a workflow and keep your team updated on progress.
